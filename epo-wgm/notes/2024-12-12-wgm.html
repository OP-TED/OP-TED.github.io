Working Group meeting

Date: 12/12/2024  
Participants: Victorio Bentivogli, Natalie Muric,
Model editor: Andreea Passare
Note editor: Achilles Dougalis

Agenda

  • #591

  • #710

  • Discuss whether this should go into the alignment section #714

  • Discuss #711

  • Take a decision for #715

  • Take a decision for #680

  • Discuss #713

  • #679

  • Discuss updates #712

Discussions

  • #726 was discussed.

    • It was mentioned that technical identifiers will not be mapped to the Ontology.

    • It was discussed that a PIN does not have a Procedure.

  • #591 was implemented.

  • #710 : OrganizationalUnit was added to the Ontology.

  • #728 was discussed, and each participant gave their view on the subject.

  • #680 was discussed. The cardinality of epo:isSubjectToProcedureSpecificTerm was relaxed to [0..*].

  • #679 was discussed: The cardinality of epo:hasAwardCriteriaStatedInProcurementDocuments was relaxed to [0..1]

  • Regarding #715:

    • It was discussed that the cardinalities of epo:refersToProcedure, and epo:refersToLot predicates of epo:Notice should be relaxed to [0..1].

    • RefersToProcedure was renamed to concernsToProcedure at the Notice subclasses level and the subproperty relationships were removed.

  • #711 was discussed and the issue was updated. It still needs to be discussed with the eForms team.

  • #713 was discussed.
