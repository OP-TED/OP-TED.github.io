A Role of an Agent who transfers the ownership of the Procurement results (goods, services or works) to the Buyer.<br/><br/>Additional information:<br/>A Role of an Agent that sells a Procurement Result (goods, services or work) to a Buyer. <br/>The Seller is bound by a Contract i.e. it has legal responsibility.<br/>The Seller may or may not be the same as the Supplier. <br/>The Seller may or may not issue the Invoice.<br/>The Seller may or may not be the owner of the credit owed by the Buyer. <br/><br/>WG acceptance 08/09/2022<br/>
