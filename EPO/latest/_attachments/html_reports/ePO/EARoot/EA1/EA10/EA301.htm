Result concerning the mini-competition that was attributed by the Awarder.<br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/>The mini-competition is the process where multiple winners or candidates of previous stages of a procedure bid for a specific procurement.<br/>It is typically used in framework agreements where the suppliers have already been pre-selected, and the mini competition is used to determine which supplier is best suited for a particular project or contract.<br/>It is also used in a Dynamic Purchasing System where the suppliers come from a list of Candidates.<br/>
