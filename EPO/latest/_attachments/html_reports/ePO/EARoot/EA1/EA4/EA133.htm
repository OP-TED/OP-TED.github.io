A Role of an Agent that awards a Contract and/or purchases items.<br/><br/>Additional information:<br/>In Public Procurement the Role of Buyer is carried out by the following types of Organisation: Contracting Authority, Contracting Entity, a defense Contractor, an international Organisation, or an organisation awarding a Contract subsidized by a Contracting Authority.<br/>In Pre-Award, the Buyer generally awards the Contract, however future purchasers may be foreseen.<br/>In Post-Award the buyer generally refers to the purchaser of items.<br/>

epo:isContractingEntity Public xsd:boolean
skos:historyNote=WG Approval 28/04/2020
Notes: Role of entities, which:<br/><br/>(a) are contracting authorities or public undertakings and which pursue one of the activities referred to in Articles 8 to 14 of the Directive 2014/25/EU.<br/><br/>(b) when they are not contracting authorities or public undertakings, have as one of their activities any of the activities referred to in Articles 8 to 14, or any combination thereof and operate on the basis of special or exclusive rights granted by a competent authority of a Member State.<br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/>The indicator is needed in order to discriminate between those contracts where the Contracting Entity acts as a Contracting Authority undergoing the limits and the rules of Directive 24 and those where it acts as a  Contracting Entity, with certain relaxed constraints.<br/>For example, the Contracting Entities have different thresholds for the application of Directive 24 if compared with Contracting Authorities.<br/>
