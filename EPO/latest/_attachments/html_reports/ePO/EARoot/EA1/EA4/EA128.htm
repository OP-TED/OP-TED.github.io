Relative concept that ties an Agent to a part they play in a given Situational Context.<br/><br/>Additional information:<br/>The classification of roles is based is based on the degree of involvement into the Procurement Process: <br/>- Primary (Procurement) Pole is directly involved in the Procurement Process;<br/>- Secondary (Procurement sub-) Role is secondary to the Procurement Process<br/>- Tertiary (Related) Role is not involved in the Procurement Process.<br/><br/>
