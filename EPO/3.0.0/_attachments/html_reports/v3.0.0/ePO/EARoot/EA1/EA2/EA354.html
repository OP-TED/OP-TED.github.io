A medium through which an Agent provides, uses or interacts in another way with a Public Service. Typical examples include online services, phone, walk-in centres etc.<br/><br/>Additional Information<br/>Software solutions and electronic devices for communication and exchange of information between buyers and economic operators.<br/>Lots may use ad-hoc electronic means of communication that are not generally available such as specific solutions for secure and dedicated communication or non-standard eCatalogues.<br/><br/>See also Directive recitals 53 to 56.<br/><br/>WG Suggestion to eForms 30/09/2019<br/><br/><br/><br/>
