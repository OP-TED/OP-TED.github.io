A collection of people organized together into a community or other social, commercial or political structure. The group has some common purpose or reason for existence which goes beyond the set of people belonging to it and can act as an Agent. Organisations are often decomposable into hierarchical structures.<br/><br/>(WG approval 23/11/2018).<br/><br/>

Notes: The type of setup recognised by law of an organisation.<br/><br/>Additional information<br/><br/>There is no formal definition of legal form in the general theory of law.
