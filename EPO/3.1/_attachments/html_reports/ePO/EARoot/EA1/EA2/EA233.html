Relative concept that ties an agent to a part they play in a given situational context.<br/><br/>Additional information:<br/>The classification of roles is based is based on the degree of involvement into the procurement process: <br/>- Primary (Procurement) role is directly involved in the procurement process;<br/>- Secondary (Procurement sub-)role is secondary to the procurement process<br/>- Tertiary (Related) role is not involved in the procurement process.<br/>
