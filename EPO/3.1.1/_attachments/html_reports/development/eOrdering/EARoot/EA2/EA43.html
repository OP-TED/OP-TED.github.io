Exchange Use Cases :<br/><br/>Case 1 [PEPPOL]:  The Seller (rejects delivery of the ordered item and) replaces an ordered item with a new item. An underlying assumption is that the Buyer tacitly accepts the substitute item. AND the Buyer updates the original order to match the suggested item changes in its system, AND does NOT resend the updated order to the Seller.<br/>If the Buyer does not accept the suggested item, then the situation is resolved as agreed in the contract, and is out of the scope of the digital exchange. For example by phone or email exchange.<br/><br/><br/>Case 2 (not in PEPPOL): The Seller (rejects delivery of the ordered item, and) recommends an alternative item. <br/>The Buyer sends a new order with new "acceptable items", which were recommended by the Seller recommends the new items instead.<br/>
