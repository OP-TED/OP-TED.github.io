Projection of an entity and all of its properties that hold in a given situation context. <br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/>The contextual projection does not have its own identity (-I), is dependent  (+D) on the described entity, and is anti-rigid (~R)<br/><br/>We say that ContextualProjection *describes* an entity.<br/><br/>Adaptation of ContextSlices pattern encoding a 4D view.<br/>http://ontologydesignpatterns.org/wiki/Submissions:Context_Slices<br/>
