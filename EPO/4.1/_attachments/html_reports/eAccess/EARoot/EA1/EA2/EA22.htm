A work in a legislative corpus. This applies to acts that have been legally enacted (whether or not they are still in force).<br/>For example, the abstract concept of the legal resource; e.g. "act 3 of 2005" (adapted from Akoma Ntoso)<br/><br/>A legal resource can represent a legal act or any component of a legal act, like an article.  Legal resources can be linked together using properties defined in the model.<br/><br/>Note that ELI ontology accommodates different point of view on what should be considered a new legal resource, or a new legal expression of the same resource. Typically, a consolidated version can be viewed, in the context of ELI, either as separate legal resource (linked to original version and previous consolidated version using corresponding ELI relations), or as a different legal expression of the same legal resource.<br/><br/>
