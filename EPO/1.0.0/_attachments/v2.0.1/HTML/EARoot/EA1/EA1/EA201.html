Information about fields not intended for publication. <br/><br/><br/>Additional Information:<br/>The non-published information may become available at a later date and may differ from one element to another within a given document.<br/><br/>Examples of fields that may not be immediately published are Winner, Tender and Procedure Lot Result, etc., e.g. for security reasons.<br/><br/>WG Approval 16/05/2019<br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/>
